Butter Naan

Home

Description

This homemade naan recipe makes soft, chewy naan with a buttery taste. It is the best I have tasted outside of an Indian restaurant.

Ingredients

  • Maida
  • Garlic
  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Water

Steps

  1. Make medium-sized balls from the dough.
  2. Flatten slightly and sprinkle some flour on the dough as well as on the rolling board.
  3. Heat a skillet or tawa or griddle and keep it on medium-high to high flame. Place the naan bread ready on the hot tawa or skillet or griddle.
  4. Grill the first side on the flame till you see some charred spots and blisters.
  5. Turn over and roast the second side too till you see some charred spots
