Paneer Butter Masala

Home

Description

This Paneer Butter Masala recipe is a rich and creamy dish of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) in a tomato, butter and cashew sauce that is known here as “makhani gravy”.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Paneer
  • Butter
  • Spices
  • Ginger Garlic

Steps

  1. Blend to a smooth tomato puree. Set aside. Don’t add any water while blending the tomatoes.
  2. Melt butter in a pan on a low heat.
  3. Add the tomato puree and stir well. Cook for 5 to 6 minutes stirring a few times.
  4. Heat a pan, add the tomato puree and all the spices
  5. After the gravy thickens to your desired consistency, then add the paneer cubes and stir gently. I keep the gravy to a medium consistency.
