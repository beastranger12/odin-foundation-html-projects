Masala Macaroni

Description

This is an indian style macaroni recipe made with tomato, cheese and lot of spices. This dish packs a lot of spices and is a favorite of mine.

Ingredients

  • Penne pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Chicken Masala
  • MSG
  • Chilli Powder

Steps

  1. Boil water, add and cook the pasta till it turns into soft and mushy
  2. Drain the pasta water and dry the pasta in a seperate container
  3. Blend the tomatoes and make into a thick paste
  4. Heat a pan, add the tomato paste and all the spices
  5. Add the pasta when the raw flavor of the spices goes away and mix to perfection
